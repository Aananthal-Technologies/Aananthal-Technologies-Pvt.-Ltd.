What Is PHMS

Your pet's health,
always watched.

PHMS is a continuous, AI-driven pet health monitoring platform. A lightweight wearable sensor attached to your pet streams vitals 24/7 to our cloud AI engine, which watches for anomalies that human observation might miss — respiratory irregularities at 3am, subtle temperature spikes, dangerous drops in activity.

When something looks off, PHMS doesn't wait. It fires an instant alert to the pet owner and their linked veterinarian, with a pre-built diagnostic context packet so the vet can act immediately — not after a long intake process.

System Architecture

How PHMS Works

Every alert starts with a sensor reading. Here's the complete data journey from pet to vet in under 5 seconds.

pets
01
Pet & Sensor
Wearable collar sensor continuously captures heart rate, temperature, SpO₂, respiratory rate, and movement vectors at 10Hz.
arrow_downward
wifi
02
Data Transmission
Encrypted BLE → Gateway → Cloud pipeline. Data is timestamped, compressed, and transmitted with <500ms end-to-end latency.
arrow_downward
storage
03
Data Processing Layer
Raw signals are cleaned, normalised, and stored in a time-series database. Baseline health profiles are maintained per individual animal.
arrow_downward
psychology
04
AI Diagnostic Engine
Multi-model ensemble (anomaly detection + species-specific classifiers) analyses incoming data against individual baselines. Confidence-scored outputs with diagnostic context.
arrow_downward
notifications_active
05a
Owner Alert
Push notification + SMS with plain-language summary. Severity-tiered from advisory to emergency.
local_hospital
05b
Vet Dashboard
Clinician receives structured diagnostic report, 24h vital history, and AI-suggested differential diagnoses — ready to act immediately.
