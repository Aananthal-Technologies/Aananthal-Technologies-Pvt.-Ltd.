What Are Industrial Drives

Smarter motors.
Lower bills.
Zero surprises.

Industrial Drive Systems are the intelligent layer between your power supply and your motors. They don't just control speed — they optimise every aspect of motor operation, eliminate energy waste, and monitor the health of your entire drive train continuously.

Aananthal's Industrial Drives add AI-driven predictive maintenance on top of precision control — detecting bearing wear, winding degradation, and load imbalances weeks before they cause a shutdown. The result: 35% energy savings, near-zero unplanned downtime, and a complete audit trail for every motor on your plant floor.
