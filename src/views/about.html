Our Story

Born from a
hunger to
build.

Aananthal Technologies was founded in Bangalore with a single conviction — that India can build world-class intelligent systems from scratch. Not just software. Not just apps. Robots. AI. Real things that interact with the physical world.

Our founders didn't come from prestigious institutes or legacy corporations. They came from curiosity, late nights, and an obsession with making things that matter. That culture never left us.

Today, we're building THANU — India's humanoid robot — alongside PHMS and Industrial Drive Systems that solve real industrial and healthcare challenges. We're just getting started.
