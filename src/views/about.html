Our Story

Born from a
hunger to
build.

Aananthal Technologies was founded in Bangalore with a single conviction — that India can build world-class intelligent systems from scratch. Not just software. Not just apps. Robots. AI. Real things that interact with the physical world.

Our founders didn't come from prestigious institutes or legacy corporations. They came from curiosity, late nights, and an obsession with making things that matter. That culture never left us.

Today, we're building THANU — India's humanoid robot — alongside PHMS and VFD solutions that solve real industrial and healthcare challenges. We're just getting started.

Milestones
2023
Founded in Bangalore
A small team with big ambitions sets up in Horamavu, Bangalore — focused on AI and robotics research.
2024
THANU R&D Begins
Humanoid robot research kicks off. Early bipedal movement prototypes and AI interaction models tested in the lab.
2024

Pet Health Management System and Variable Frequency Drive products enter development, addressing real-world challenges.
2025
Team Grows to 20+
Engineers, researchers, and builders from across India join the mission. No degrees required — just passion.
2026
Gait Research Milestone
