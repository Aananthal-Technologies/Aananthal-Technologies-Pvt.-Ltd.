Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Aananthal Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("Aananthal", "we", "us", or "our") is a technology company registered in India, headquartered at Horamavu Agara Main Road, Bangalore, Karnataka 560043. We operate this website and our associated products and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including when you:

  • Fill out the contact or collaboration form on our website
  • Apply for a career opportunity and upload a resume
  • Send us an email or reach out directly

This may include your name, email address, phone number, portfolio URL, and any documents you submit.

We also collect anonymous usage data through Vercel Analytics and Speed Insights to improve our website's performance. This data does not identify you personally.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and contact form submissions
  • Review and process career applications
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Form submissions and career applications are stored securely in our Supabase database with industry-standard encryption. We retain this data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected.

Uploaded files (resumes, CVs) are stored in memory during processing and are not persisted beyond the review process unless explicitly required.

5. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. Performance analytics (Vercel) may set anonymous session identifiers. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
